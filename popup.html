ReNews

ReNews is here to help you analyze and balance political bias in news articles. Here’s how it works:

  • Detects when you visit a news article automatically.
  • Displays a hover panel on the right side of your screen.
  • Extracts the main content for analysis.
  • Analyzes bias level and political leaning on our server.
  • Shows the analysis results, including bias level and leaning.
  • If bias is found, provides an option to neutralize content for a balanced view.

Features

  • Bias Meter: Indicates the level of detected bias.
  • Neutralization Option: Click to view a neutralized version of biased content.
  • Adjustable Panel: Toggle the panel to minimize it and bring it back into view
